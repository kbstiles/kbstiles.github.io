Main Premise

We started out with a base game that we created during practice. From there we needed to make changes to the game based on a point system. We needed to reach a total point change of 100, with at least 2 beginner options (which give 5 points each), 2 intermediate options (which give 10 points each) and 2 advanced options (which give 15 points each) to start off with. From there we were given free reign on whichever changes we wanted to make out of the options left. They just needed to add up to 100 points. After all the changes we end up with a completely revamped game, with changed images, colors, added enemies and obstacles. After reaching a certain score the user wins and if their score reaches zero from hitting to many obstacles they lose.
